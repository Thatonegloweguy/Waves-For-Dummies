Waves For Dummies

   by @Geobeasty

  Waves is a hairstyle for curly hair in which the curls are brushed and/or combed and flattened
out, creating a ripple-like pattern.

This hairstyle can be achieved with a short-cropped haircut on top and frequent brushing
and/or combing of the curls/hair(which trains the curls to flatten out), as well as wearing a durag.




Step One

Hair Care







The First Step is to obviously is to have some hair, make sure your hair is in good health before starting this process, this includes basic hair hygiene.

After acknowledging this vital information then you can continue unto the next step.




Step Two

What You've been waiting for







The Second Step is to start the process, wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair nice and soft
(it's optional not to use any products) make sure your hair is damp but not soaking wet.




Step Three

Moisturizer Time







The Third Step is to use a water-based or butter-based product to moisturize your hair while itâ€™s still damp.
Rub the product in with your hands to lay down the waves




Step Four

Get to Brushing







The Fourth Step is Get a good brush (medium bristles work well) and brush your hair in the direction of your natural growth pattern or in a swirl pattern, depending on the type of waves you want and make sure to flatten out your hair to get that desired look. If you want to make sure to you get those beautiful waves you favorite just remember consistency is key.

If you followed these steps from this know-how guide, I'm pretty you got your waves you hoped for
